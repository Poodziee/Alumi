"Why is Sleep Necessary?"

There are two main factors: Overall Health, and Emotional Well-Being.

Overall Health
- Sleep provides plays a vital role in maintaining physical health and well-being, especially for college students. This factor lowers the risk of diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease.

Emotional Well-Being
- According to researchers, sleep is linked to improved mood, and reduce stress levels in emotional challenges. College students face numerous challenges along the way, and prioritizing sleep is the way to go.

We should always prioritize sleep at night, ensuring that your health is at its fullest potential in order for you to live a wealthy life!

Sleep is a good drug, enjoy each and every moment!
