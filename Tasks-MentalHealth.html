"Why is Mental Health important?"

Mental Health is important to every college student as they face academic challenges which can be difficult.

It is Influencial
- Mental Health plays a significant role on how you act and think, being a beneficial asset towards your goals. However, it can also disrupt your plans if influenced negatively.

It is Reforming
- Mental health can change who you are, and what you are. With the right mentality, students can focus on tasks and could improve their skills and abilities.

Taking care of your mental health is necessary, letting you learn from past mistakes. It is what makes us who we are!

Remember, you are loved. You are you and we love you!
