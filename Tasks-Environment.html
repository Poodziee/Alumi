"Why is Environment Important?"

There are three main factors:
Air Quality, Water, and Nature itself.

Air Quality
- Air is an important factor towards our health, especially for our respiratory system. Ensuring the quality of air provides you a refreshing experience throughout your day.

Water
- Water is the source of life, meaning that our overall health and hygiene relies on this alone. With clean water, our bodies are nourished to help us on our daily tasks and activities.

Nature
- Nature is not only an ecosystem, but a relaxing home for our well-being. It provides us numerous benefits that impact our mental health, as well as relieving pain and stress from our body.

Clean Environment, Healthy Body!
