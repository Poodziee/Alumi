"Why is Exercise Necessary?"

There are two main factors: Overall Health, and Mental Well-Being.

Overall Health
- Exercise is essential for maintaining physical health and well-being. This is essential for college students who may spend long hours sitting in lectures, studying at desks, or working on assignments.

Mental Well-Being
- Exercise also plays a significant role in mental well-being and self-esteem. College students may experience academic pressures and challenges which can hinder their performance on academics. This will improve their mood and self-esteem once they are encouraged to exercise.

Regular exercise improves your overall health and well-being, and being a college student can balance the academic struggles and challenges one face.
