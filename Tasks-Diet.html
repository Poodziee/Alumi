"Why is Diet Necessary?"

There are three main factors: Nutrient Intake, Energy Source, and Brain Function.

Nutrient Intake
- Food provides essential nutrients that are vital for maintaining optimal health.

Energy Source
- Food provides the energy necessary to sustain our overall activities within the day.

Brain Function
- Food is essential for optimal brain function and cognitive performance because of its nutrients such as vitamins and minerals.
