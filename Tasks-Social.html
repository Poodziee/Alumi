"Why is Social Health Important?"

There are two main factors:
Social Support, and Community Life.

Social Support
- Being socially active encourages us to have positive relationships with our family, friends, and other people. It lessens the risk of negative mental thoughts, and improves our quality of life.

Community Life
- Participating community-related tasks improves our identity, growth, and purpose. Opportunities also arise which may be beneficial for us as we help our surroundings, our people, and our community.

Overall,
- Our social health impacts our actions and minds, improving them through our interactions with people. It promotes our connections and well-being, as well as the environment around us.
